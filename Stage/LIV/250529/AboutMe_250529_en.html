I've been a bit too obsessed with Animal Crossing lately, and as an iOS developer, I thought it might be nice to have an app to record interactions with the villagers on my island—so I built this simple little app.

I occasionally return to tweak a few details, and recently added a new data feature. It's mainly something I use myself, but if it helps other players too, that would be great.

Since the App Store doesn't support direct donation models, I chose to place certain limitations on data visibility. Users can choose to watch ads or pay to unlock these limitations as a way to support development.

In any case, I hope this app can be helpful in your experience. If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to me.

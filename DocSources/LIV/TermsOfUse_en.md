

##### Subscriptions or Non-Consumable In-App Purchases

Some features require a subscription or a non-consumable in-app purchase to be unlocked and used.

The app uses the App Store's payment system, and payments will be processed through your Apple ID account. When you subscribe, you will see the subscription details in your Apple ID account after confirming your choice. Please note that subscriptions automatically renew at the end of each billing cycle. If you wish to cancel your subscription, please do so at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.

To cancel your subscription, follow these steps:
1. Open the "Settings" app on your device.
2. Tap on your Apple ID at the top of the screen.
3. Select "Subscriptions."
4. Choose the subscription you wish to cancel.
5. Tap "Cancel Subscription."

You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime in the Account Settings of the App Store.

Refunds should be handled through Apple's official process via your Apple ID account. The developer can only assist with refunds as allowed by the App Store.
